How to determine a species

  1. Tap the "Search"-Button below
  2. Select the animal-family that your "Thing" could belong to
  3. Tap on each characteristic you know about your "Thing" and select the option which fits best
  4. Click on "Find" and find out what your "Thing" really is

What are animal-families, characters and species?

Let's take the Collie for example: The animal-family of a Collie is Dogs, the possible characters for dogs are hair-length, hair-color or the size and the Collie himself is the species.
